Our Projects

In FilRobotics, we believe in Filipino inventiveness and resourcefulness in using Robotics solution to the pressing challenges in the society.

ROBOTractor

The primary objective of the Robotractor project is to develop a robotic platform that will convert ordinary hand tractor to operate autonomously without the need for our local farmer to operate it manually up and down the field.

Learn more

AQUADrone

The AQUADRONE aims to provide a convenient way of collecting water for quality monitoring in lakes and other bodies of water in the country using autonomous unmanned aerial vehicle.

Learn more

LISA Robot

The LISA robot or Logistic Indoor Service Assistant robot was developed primary as an immediate response to COVID19 pandemic.
