LISA Robot

Logistic Indoor Service Assistant

The LISA robot or Logistic Indoor Service Assistant robot was developed primary as an immediate response to COVID19 pandemic. The first versions of the robot were radio controlled and capable of delivering food and medicines. The Department of Science and Technology- Philippine Council for Health Research and Development (DOST-PCHRD) took notice of the simple and effective technology and will fund the development of the fully automated version. This will be in partnership with different private and public hospitals in the country.
