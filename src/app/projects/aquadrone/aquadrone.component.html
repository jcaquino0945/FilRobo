AQUADrone Project

Unmanned Aerial Vehicle

The AQUADRONE aims to provide a convenient way of collecting water for quality monitoring in lakes and other bodies of water in the country using autonomous unmanned aerial vehicle. The project is funded by Philippine Council for Industry, Energy and Emerging Technology Research and Development DOST-PCIEERD and in partnership with the Bureau of Fisheries and Aquatic Resources, BFAR and Philippine Society of Fresh Water Science
