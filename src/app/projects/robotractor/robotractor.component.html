ROBOTractor Project

GPS Guided Hand Tractor

The primary objective of the Robotractor project is to develop a robotic platform that will convert ordinary hand tractor to operate autonomously without the need for our local farmer to operate it manually up and down the field. This project was funded by Philippine Council for Agriculture, Aquatic and Natural Resources ResearchÂ DOST-PCAARRD and in partnership with the Philippine rice research institute or PhiRice and International rice research institute or IRRI. This will be the first GPS guided hand tractor in our country.
